About Us

“Visualize this thing you want. See it, feel it, believe in it. Make your mental blueprint and begin.”

You have within you the capability to accomplish greatness. Whatever your fitness goal, whether it’s to lose weight, build strength, get competition ready, or just get healthier, we have the tools you need to succeed.

CTX Fitness is a full service fitness center, complete with top of the line equipment and superior coaches and trainers. It is our mission to create a workout environment that promotes confidence and success, helping you to achieve your personal fitness goals.

We have a range of options and services to meet your unique needs - regardless of your age or fitness level. CTX Fitness has both indoor and outdoor training areas, complete with all the standard gym equipment - free weights, a cardio room, weight machines, custom squat racks, and more. We also have specialized equipment to further challenge your body and help you prepare for competitions.

CTX members compete in everything from Strongman to Crossfit, mud races, Power Lifting, Olympic Lifting, savage races, marathons, Physique and Bodybuilding competitions, and more. At CTX Fitness, we are invested in your success, supporting you every step of the way, celebrating your victories – and preparing you for your next challenge. We are committed to our clients, keeping you injury free and on track to surpass your goals.

We offer standard gym memberships as well as a variety of classes and packages – there is something for everyone, whatever your goal, situation, or budget. Our specialties include:

  • Personalized Meal Plans & Nutritional Coaching
  • Strength & Endurance Training
  • Resistance Band Training
  • Power Lifting, Olypmic Lifting & Strongman
  • Motivation & Accountability
  • Customized Workout Programs
  • Competition Prep
  • Weight Loss
  • Sculpting/"Toning"
  • Cross-Training

CTX Fitness is more than just a gym; for most of our members, it’s a second home. It’s a victory ground, the place for you to shatter your limitations and achieve your best!

What are you waiting for? Call now or come by to get started – 3 months from now you’re going to wish you’d started today! Just imagine, a year from now you could be in the best shape of your life!
